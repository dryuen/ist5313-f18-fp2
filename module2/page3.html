Understanding and Analysis of Literary Texts

Strategy #1: take a look at the image and captions first

Step 1

- Read any titles and images
- Any informative text will always have an image or images and some captions explaining the image.
- By looking at this information we can make some guesses.

Step 2

- Go to the questions and try to find and answer any question that you can answer without reading the passage.
