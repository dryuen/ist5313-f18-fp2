This module will work on your Understanding and Anaylsis of Informational Texts. This program was designed to teach strategies to help you understand how to play the game and pass the test. You need 43% correct to pass. You have 5 hours to take the EOC, within those 5 hours you must work on a two part test (reading and writing). Usually, this section of the test will have 10 - 12 questions. Please study this module thoroughly and then take our test to gauge your improvement.
