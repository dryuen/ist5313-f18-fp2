Strategy 5: Note taking / coding

Previous | Next
Since this is a timed test, taking notes helps prevent you from re-reading and wasting precious time, use the following symbols to "code" the reading. Hint: You can have multiple symbols within the same paragraph.
  1. # for any message or lesson the author may want to teach the reader.
  2. ! for any important information.
  3. $ for any numbers in the text (dates,times,or money).
  4. ? for any questions you may have in the reading or a word you may not understand.
  5. ❤ for any emotion in the reading or any emotional connection you might make.
