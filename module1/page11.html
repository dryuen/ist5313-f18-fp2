Strategy 5: Note taking / coding

AUDIO:"Since this is a timed test, taking notes helps prevent you from re-reading and wasting precious time, use the following symbols to "code" the reading. Hint: You can have multiple symbols within the same paragraph.

Check out the image. In particular, pay attention to the bullet points. When an image has bullet points, it is important to read each one carefully."

  1. # for any message or lesson the author may want to teach the reader
  2. ! for any important information
  3. $ for any numbers in the text (dates,times,or money)
  4. ? for any questions you may have in the reading or a word you may not understand.
  5. ❤ for any emotion in the reading or any emotional connection you might make
